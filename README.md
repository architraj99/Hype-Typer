# Hype Typer

An arcade style falling word typing game built by me

 Play it live - https://architraj99.github.io/Hype-Typer

Words drop from the top of the screen. Type them before they cross the shield line. Miss too many and it's game over.


## How to Play

- Words fall toward the red shield line at the bottom
- Type a word and it clears as soon as you complete it (no Enter needed)
- Miss a word and your shield takes −20 damage
- Shield hits 0 - game over

### Scoring

| Action | Points |
|---|---|
| Clear a word | 100 + (streak × 10) |
| Overcharge clear | 50 per word cleared |

### Streak & Speed

Every 5 words cleared in a row increases fall speed by +0.2× (max 3×) 
Break your streak by letting a word through.

### Overcharge (TAB)

click the TAB button to instantly clear all active words. Awards 50 points per word removed. Goes on a  10 second cooldown after use.
